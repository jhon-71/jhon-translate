# Privacy Policy for Jhon Translate

**Effective Date:** May 2026

## Overview
Jhon Translate is a lightweight, open-source browser extension designed to provide bilingual webpage translations. We believe in absolute privacy and minimal intrusion.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.** 
The extension operates entirely locally within your browser, with the following exceptions:
- The text you explicitly choose to translate is sent securely to the public Google Translate API to retrieve the translation.
- Your target language preference is saved locally on your device using the browser's `chrome.storage` API. This data never leaves your device.

## Analytics & Tracking
We do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party monitoring tools.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/jhon-71/jhon-translate/issues).
